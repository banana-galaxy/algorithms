Self Promotion is only allowed in #Self-Promotion. You can only send a message in that group once a week.
Self promotion is defined as sharing information that promotes you or your business. Some examples of self promotion include:
Asking for people to visit, share, or follow a group, page, or website.
Asking for people to like a post considered self-promotional.
A post containing link(s) or text that lead to your page, group or website.
A post containing your company or page logo.
A video post asking users to follow your page, group or website.
Sharing a post that leads the user to your page, group, or website.
